Narcos Season 3

The third season of "Narcos" will be focusing on the aftermath of Medellin Cartel and the rise of Cali Cartel and will start with the latter surpassing the former as a result of the death of Pablo Escobar. The storyline will be about the four Cali godfathers Gilberto, Miguel, Pancho Herrera, and "Chepe." As with the fate of Pablo Escobar, their endings will just be as bad, but how the characters get there is the fun part.
